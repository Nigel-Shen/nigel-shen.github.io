ER=EPR? A Discern from a Philosophical Perspective

Mentored by Professor Jonathan Bain.

Quantum entangled (“EPR”, or Einstein-Podolsky-Rosen) state are connected by an Einstein-Rosen (“ER”) wormhole. Our project’s goals were to assess the motivations for “ER=EPR”, including AdS/CFT theory and the Black Hole Firewall Problem, and to construct a conceptual map that relates characteristics of wormholes to characteristics of entanglement.
